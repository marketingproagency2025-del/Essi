Frequently Asked Questions

What services does MarketingPro offer?

MarketingPro covers social media management, advertising campaigns on Meta and Google, website creation, SEO, sales funnels, photo and video editing, photorealistic renders, and print catalogues.

Where is MarketingPro based?

We are a remote-first digital marketing agency working with clients across Italy, the rest of Europe and the United States.

Do you only generate leads, or do you contact them too?

We generate the leads and then call and qualify them ourselves, so your sales team only speaks with people who are ready to buy or to ask for a quote.

Which advertising platforms do you use?

Most of our paid campaigns run on Meta (Facebook and Instagram) and Google, chosen based on where each client's customers actually spend their time.
