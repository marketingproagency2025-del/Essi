About MarketingPro

MarketingPro is a digital marketing agency for businesses across Italy, Europe and the United States. We help you bring in real customers, not just leads, by generating, calling and qualifying every lead before it reaches you.

Our Story

MarketingPro started in the Cyclades, a place of white walls and open blue where things stay simple. Working there, we kept seeing how cluttered and noisy modern marketing had become, and how little of that noise actually brought in customers.

So we built MarketingPro around one idea: bring that same clarity to the way a business grows.

Over more than ten years we've learned that the work that pays off is rarely the loudest. A good strategy is a clear route from a stranger seeing your ad to a signed contract, and building that route is what we do, in English and Italian, for clients across Italy, Europe and the US.

How We Work

One team for the whole picture, with qualification running through all of it.

We run the whole picture, from social media and paid ads to websites, SEO, sales funnels, video, renders and catalogues, so the parts actually work together. The thread through everything is qualification: we generate the leads, then call and filter them ourselves, so your sales team only spends time on people who are ready to talk business. That means fewer wasted hours and more signed work.

If you want to see how we think about each service, our marketing blog takes it one topic at a time.
